# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Contributors, maintainers, and participants in this project commit to a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, experience level, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Constructive behaviors:**
- Respectful, technically-focused discussion
- Welcoming feedback on detection coverage, scoring methods, and emulation logic
- Crediting sources and prior work

**Unacceptable behaviors:**
- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language
- Repurposing this project's research for offensive or malicious use
- Sharing any real credentials, exploit payloads, or victim system data in issues or PRs

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, and any community channels — and extends to public representation of the project.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to **contact@sudosecurityconsulting.com**. All reports will be reviewed and responded to within five business days. Maintainers reserve the right to remove content and ban contributors who violate these standards.
